Haibo Jin

Engineer@Alibaba | PhD@HKUST

Bio

I am a senior algorithm engineer at Alibaba DAMO Academy, Hangzhou. I obtained my PhD from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) in 2025, under the supervision of Prof. Hao Chen. Previously, I was an algorithm engineer at the Inception Institute of Artificial Intelligence (IIAI) in Abu Dhabi from 2019 to 2021.

My research interests include computer vision, medical image analysis, and vision-language modeling.
